Buttons

Besides normal Foundation buttons, you can also specify green and pink buttons.

Primary Buttons:

.button

.btn-green

.btn-green

Secondary Buttons:

.btn-border

.btn-hollow-green

Call to Actions:

You can add an arrow to your button by adding the class .cta

.btn-green.cta

For CTAs on form submit buttons, wrap the submit button in a class of .button.cta (or something similar) and then don't put any further classes on the submit input element itself.
