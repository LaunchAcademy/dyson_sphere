Typography Helper Classes

These helper classes will allow you to apply consistent styling to different types of text you might want to use.

For meta information - .meta-info:

Christina Koller - June 29, 2015 at 3:15pm 
<span class="meta-info">Christina Koller - June 29, 2015 at 3:15pm</span>

For blue text color - .blue-text:

Example Blue Header

<h3 class="blue-text">Example Blue Header</h3>
